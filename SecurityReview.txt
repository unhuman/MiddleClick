  - Private MultitouchSupport framework: An undocumented Apple system framework, standard practice for trackpad utilities.
  - CGEvent taps + Accessibility APIs: The core mechanism for mouse button emulation — exactly what the app claims to do, and it requires
  your explicit permission grant.
